Teaching Approach

My approach to teaching is grounded in the belief that learning should be an active, engaging process. I strive to create an environment where students feel comfortable asking questions, making mistakes, and exploring ideas.

Active Learning

Encouraging hands-on experience through projects and interactive discussions.

Collaborative Environment

Fostering peer learning and teamwork in problem-solving.

Real-world Applications

Connecting theoretical concepts to practical, industry-relevant problems.
