Publications

My research has been published in top-tier conferences and journals in machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data science. Below is a selection of my recent publications organized by year.

2024

"Interpretable Neural Networks through Attention Mechanisms"

Manuel S. Mathew, Sarah Johnson, David Lee

International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML 2024)

This paper presents novel approaches to interpreting deep neural networks through attention mechanisms and gradient-based visualization techniques. We demonstrate significant improvements in model transparency without sacrificing performance across multiple benchmarks.

"Robust Learning Algorithms for Adversarial Environments"

Manuel S. Mathew, Alex Chen, Maria Rodriguez

Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS 2024)

We propose novel algorithms for robust machine learning that maintain high performance under adversarial conditions. Our approach demonstrates superior resilience compared to existing methods across various attack scenarios.

"Ethical Frameworks for AI Decision Making"

Manuel S. Mathew, Jennifer Kim, Robert Taylor

Journal of Artificial Intelligence Research (JAIR 2023)

This work establishes comprehensive ethical frameworks for AI systems, focusing on bias detection and mitigation strategies. We provide practical guidelines for implementing ethical considerations in AI development workflows.

Manuel S. Mathew, Lisa Wang, James Brown

IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering (2023)
