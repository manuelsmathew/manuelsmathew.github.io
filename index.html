About Me

Welcome to my academic portfolio. I am a Professor of Computer Science with over 15 years of experience in machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data science. My research focuses on developing innovative algorithms that bridge the gap between theoretical computer science and practical applications.

I am passionate about advancing the field through rigorous research, mentoring the next generation of computer scientists, and translating complex technical concepts into accessible knowledge for students and the broader community.

Research Overview

My research spans multiple areas of computer science, with a particular focus on:

  • Machine Learning and Deep Learning
  • Artificial Intelligence Ethics
  • Data Science and Analytics
  • Human-Computer Interaction

I have published over 60 papers in top-tier conferences and journals, with over 2,950 citations across my work.
